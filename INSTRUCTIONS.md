# 🎵 Vocal Class Manager — Complete User Guide
### For Aunty Adele's Vocal Classes · Sri Lanka

---

## 🌐 Your Website Link

**https://auntys-vocal-classes.web.app**

Save this link on your phone. You can open it on any phone, tablet, or computer — no app download needed. It works on iPhone, Android, and any laptop or PC.

**Tip for iPhone/Android:** Open the link in your browser, tap the Share button, then tap "Add to Home Screen" — it will appear like an app icon on your phone!

## 📱 How to Navigate

### On a Phone (Android or iPhone)
At the **bottom of the screen** you will see 4 buttons:
- 🏠 **Home** — Dashboard (overview of everything)
- 👤 **Students** — Add and manage students
- 📅 **Attend** — Mark attendance
- 💰 **Fees** — Record and track payments

Tap any of these to switch between sections.

### On a Laptop or Computer
At the **top of the screen** you will see the same 4 tabs:
- **Dashboard · Students · Attendance · Fees**

Click any tab to switch.

## 🏙️ Step 1 — Choosing Your City (Splash Screen)

Every time you open the website, you will see a screen with **3 city cards**:

| City | Province | Class Day |
|------|----------|-----------|
| 🌊 Galle | Southern Province | Every Thursday |
| 🌿 Ja-Ela | Western Province | Every Tuesday |
| 🏙️ Colombo | Capital City | Every Thursday |

1. **Tap the city** you want to manage today
2. A ✓ checkmark will appear on the selected city
3. Tap the **"Enter [City] Classes →"** button at the bottom
4. The app will open for that city

**To switch cities later:** Tap the city name buttons in the top right corner of the header (Galle / Ja-Ela / Colombo) at any time.

## 📊 Section 1 — Dashboard (Home)

This is your **overview screen**. It shows everything at a glance for the city you selected.

### What you will see:

**Month Picker**
At the top right, there is a "Month" selector. Change it to see data for any past or current month.

**Monthly Report Button**
Two buttons are always visible:
- **"Email Report"** — Sends the full monthly report to your email AND downloads an Excel file
- **"Download Excel"** — Just downloads the Excel file without sending an email

*(In the last 3 days of the month, a special reminder banner appears with these buttons highlighted)*

**City Switcher Cards**
Three cards showing Galle, Ja-Ela, and Colombo. The active city is highlighted in dark blue. Tap any card to switch to that city. Each card shows:
- Number of students
- Class day (Tuesday / Thursday)
- Total fees collected that month

**Schedule Banner**
Shows which dates classes are scheduled for the selected month, and how many have been held so far. Past dates are shown as filled dark blue circles, upcoming dates are shown as empty circles.

**Summary Cards**
5 coloured cards showing:
- 🎤 Total Students in the city
- 📅 Classes held this month (e.g. "3/4" means 3 out of 4 held)
- 💰 Monthly fees collected
- 🗓️ Annual fees collected
- ⏳ Number of students who haven't paid yet

**Student Attendance Progress**
A list of all students with a coloured progress bar showing their attendance rate for the month:
- 🟢 Green = 75% or above (Excellent)
- 🟡 Yellow = 50–74% (Needs attention)
- 🔴 Red = Below 50% (At risk)

**All Locations Overview**
At the bottom, a summary of all 3 cities side by side — students, fees collected, and pending payments for each.

## 👩‍🎤 Section 2 — Students

This is where you **add new students and view their profiles**.

### Adding a New Student

1. Go to the **Students** tab
2. Fill in:
   - **Full Name** *(required)* — e.g. Nimal Perera
   - **Phone Number** *(optional)* — e.g. 0771234567
   - **Location** *(required)* — Select Galle, Ja-Ela, or Colombo
3. Tap **"Save Student"**
4. A green success message will appear at the top

### Searching for a Student

Use the **search bar** at the top right of the student list. Type any part of the name or phone number to filter the list instantly.

### Viewing a Student's Profile

Each student has a **"Profile"** button (eye icon) next to their name. Tap it to open a detailed profile showing:

- **Name and phone** (with an Edit button to update them)
- **Location and class day**
- **When they joined**
- **Total classes attended**
- **Overall attendance rate** with a colour bar
- **Last 6 months attendance heatmap** — each month shown as a coloured box (green = good, red = poor)
- **Full payment history** — every payment ever recorded for that student

**To edit a student's name or phone:**
1. Open their Profile
2. Tap **"Edit Profile"**
3. Change the name or phone number
4. Tap **"Save Changes"**

### Deleting a Student

Each student row has a 🗑️ trash icon. Tap it, then confirm by tapping **"Yes"**. This permanently removes the student and cannot be undone.

## 📅 Section 3 — Attendance

This is where you **mark who came to class and who didn't**. It works in 2 steps.

### Step 1 — Confirm the Date

When you open Attendance, you will first see a date confirmation screen.

**Quick date buttons:** The app automatically shows all the scheduled class dates for the current month (Tuesdays for Ja-Ela, Thursdays for Galle and Colombo). Tap the correct date button — it will highlight in dark blue.

- Dates that have already passed are shown as white buttons (you can tap them)
- Today's date has a green "Today" badge
- Future dates are greyed out (you cannot tap them)

**Manual date picker:** If you need to pick a specific date not shown, use the date picker below the buttons.

**Date confirmation box:** After selecting a date, a box shows the full date. If it is a scheduled class day, it shows ✅ in green. If it is not a scheduled day, it shows ⚠️ in yellow as a warning.

Tap **"Yes, mark attendance for this date"** to proceed.

### Step 2 — Mark Each Student

You will now see all students for that city with two buttons each:

- **✔ (green)** — Student was **Present**
- **✘ (red)** — Student was **Absent**

Tap the correct button for each student. The row will turn green for present and red for absent immediately.

**Progress bar** at the top shows how many students you have marked so far (e.g. "5 of 8 marked — 62%").

**"All Present" button** — If everyone came, tap this button to mark all students as present at once.

**"Done — Save Attendance" button** at the bottom turns green only when every student is marked. Tap it to finish. A celebration screen will appear showing the final count.

**To go back and change the date:** Tap the ← back arrow in the top left.

## 💰 Section 4 — Fees

This is where you **record payments and track who has paid**.

### Recording a Payment

At the top of the Fees section:

1. **Select Payment Type:**
   - **Monthly** — Rs. 4,500 per month (default)
   - **Annual** — Rs. 12,000 per year (default)
   Tap the toggle to switch between them. The amount will automatically change.

2. **Select Student** — Type the student's name in the search box and tap their name from the dropdown list.

3. **Select Month** — Choose which month this payment is for.

4. **Amount (Rs.)** — The default amount is pre-filled (Rs. 4,500 for monthly, Rs. 12,000 for annual). You can change it if needed.

5. **Deduction (Rs.)** — If you are giving a discount or deduction, enter the amount here (e.g. 500). The net amount will be shown automatically.

6. Tap **"Save — Rs. [amount]"** to record the payment.

### Stats Cards

4 coloured cards at the top show:
- 💙 Total amount collected this month
- 🟣 Number of annual payments
- 🟢 Number of monthly payments
- 🟡 Number of students still pending

### Pending Payments

A yellow/amber section shows all students who **have NOT paid yet** for the selected month.

Each pending student has a **"Mark Paid"** button. Tap it to expand a quick payment form right there — select type, adjust amount/deduction, and confirm without going to the main form.

### Paid This Month

A green section shows all students who **have already paid**, with:
- Their name
- Payment type badge (Monthly / Annual)
- Any deduction applied
- The date it was recorded
- The amount paid

## 📧 Monthly Report

At the end of each month (or any time you want), you can generate a full report.

### How to Send the Report

1. Go to **Dashboard (Home)**
2. Tap **"Email Report"**
3. A preview popup will appear showing:
   - Where the email is being sent (your email address)
   - What is included in the report
   - A preview of the email text
4. Tap **"Send Email + Download"**
5. Two things happen automatically:
   - An **Excel file** downloads to your device with 7 sheets (Summary + Attendance + Payments for each city)
   - An **email** is sent to your inbox with the full text summary

### Download Only (No Email)

Tap **"Download Excel"** to just get the Excel file without sending an email.

### What the Excel Report Contains

| Sheet | Contents |
|-------|----------|
| Summary | All 3 cities — total students, fees collected, pending count |
| Galle Attendance | Every student, every class date, Present/Absent |
| Galle Payments | Every payment recorded, amount, type, date |
| Ja-Ela Attendance | Same for Ja-Ela |
| Ja-Ela Payments | Same for Ja-Ela |
| Colombo Attendance | Same for Colombo |
| Colombo Payments | Same for Colombo |

## 💡 Important Tips

**Your data is saved automatically**
Everything is saved to the cloud (Firebase) the moment you tap a button. You do not need to press any "Save All" button. Even if you close the app, all data will be there when you open it again.

**Works offline? Partially**
The app needs an internet connection to save and load data. If you lose connection, a red error message will appear. Reconnect and try again.

**Green messages = success ✅**
Whenever something saves correctly, a green message appears at the top of the screen.

**Red messages = something went wrong ❌**
If a red message appears, check your internet connection and try again.

**Switching cities**
You can switch between Galle, Ja-Ela, and Colombo at any time using:
- The city name buttons in the top right corner of the header
- The city cards on the Dashboard

**The website is free forever**
This website runs on Google Firebase's free plan. There are no monthly fees. It will stay online permanently.
